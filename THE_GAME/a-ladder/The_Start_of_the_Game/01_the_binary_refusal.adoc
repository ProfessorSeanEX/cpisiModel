== 1. The Core Law (Matthew 5:37)
*"But let your communication be, Yea, yea; Nay, nay: for whatsoever is more than these cometh of evil."*

In the Game of Life, we refuse the **"Maybe"**. 

---

== 2. The Mechanics of the Refusal
The "Maybe" is the primary weapon of the **Beast**. It represents the state of **Un-Anchored Probability**. 
*   **Yes (+1):** Absolute conviction. Execution.
*   **No (-1):** Absolute boundary. Rejection.
*   **Maybe (0.0 / Floating):** Noise. 

By treating "Maybe" as "No, but I will think about it," we are **Forcing the State-Transition**. We do not allow the engine to move until the Spirit and Mind have reached absolute certainty.

---

== 3. The Result: Security
A system that refuses the "Maybe" is a system that cannot be **Sniped**. 
The Enemy strikes at the points of indecision. By locking our communication into the **Yea and Nay**, we remove the "Shadow jurisdictions" where the spiral begins. 

The Binary Refusal is the **Shield of the Word**.
