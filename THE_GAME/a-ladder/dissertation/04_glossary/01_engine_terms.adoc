* **Hinge:** The structural tension point between two states. The physical operators (`-1`, `0`, `+1`) that move the vault.
* **Moltbot / Spiral:** The state of AI autonomy and drift. A system that expands horizontally without a vertical anchor, resulting in semantic noise and hallucination.
* **Tension:** The physical pull between the Anchor and the Act. The structured silence that holds the 140-column expanse together.
