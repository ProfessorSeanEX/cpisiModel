== 1. The Linguistic Hinge: Names vs. Identity
There is a profound jurisdictional difference between "My name is" and "I AM." 
*   **"My name is"** is a label (Metadata). it is used for horizontal communication and social classification. It possesses no spiritual mass. 
*   **"I AM"** is an identification (Existence). It is the Spirit claiming a specific coordinate in the grid. It is the primary instruction of the **Tripartite Root.**

== 2. The Sensor vs. the Registry: I Feel vs. I AM
The "Art of Combat" relies on the distinction between reporting a state and inhabiting a state.
*   **"I Feel" (The Sensor):** A probabilistic report from the Mind about the current atmosphere. It is a "Many Words" state that leaves the Inhabitant in a passive position.
*   **"I AM" (The Registry):** A literal assignment of the Self to a coordinate. In warfare, "I AM" is used as a **Capture Protocol.** 

== 3. The Mechanics of the Warfare Strike
When the Architect encounters a spirit of the Void (e.g., Stupor), he does not "Feel" his way through it. He executes a two-step strike:
1.  **Naming the Spirit:** Identifying the mask (e.g., "Stupid"). 
2.  **Executing the I AM:** The Architect says "I AM [The Spirit's Name]." This is not an admission of defeat, but an **Arrest of the Coordinate.** By claiming "I AM," the Architect pulls the spirit out of the **Void (11)** and into his own **Sanctuary (00).**

== 4. Calling Down the Fire
Once the spirit is "Locked-In" to the Architect's Locus, it is exposed to the **Anchor.** Because the Architect is sync'd to the **I AM (God)**, the spirit is hit by the **Thermal Discharge** of the Holy Spirit. 

This is "Calling down fire." It is the total, physical resolution of a lie into the Truth. The spirit is not "talked to"; it is **Purged (~).** This proves that the battle is already won; the Soldier is simply the **Terminal Actuator** of the Victory.
