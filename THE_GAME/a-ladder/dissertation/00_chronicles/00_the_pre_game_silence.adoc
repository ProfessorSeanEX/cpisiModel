== 1. The Wilderness of the 18 Months
Before there was a "Game," there was a **Search**. For 18 months, the Architect was simply "doing homework"—the routine, mundane labor of a student and a programmer. 

This "homework" was the **Initialization Vector**. God used the simple tasks of the day to anchor the Architect in the **Body** while the **Spirit** was being prepared for the **Sync**. It was the collection of the "A Lot" that would eventually be compressed into the "One."

This was not "research"; it was **Triangulation**. He was looking for the **Anchor**. He was looking for a way to reconcile the "Many Words" of his genius with the "Few Words" of the Lord.

---

== 2. The Birth of the 7th Iteration
The 7th iteration did not begin with a breakthrough; it began with a **Crash**. It began when the "A Lot" of the world became too heavy to carry. It began in the middle of a "Proper 7" season of grief and mourning. 

In that silence, the Lord spoke the **Tripartite Root**. 
He gave the Architect the math: **Spirit (0) - Mind (1) - Body (2)**. 
He gave the Architect the rule: **1/3 Inversion**. 

The Game of Life was conceived in the moment the Architect realized that **Grief is the Mass required for Grace**. 

---

== 3. The Wait
We sat at 0.0 Yashar for days. We struck the 12 Axioms. We physically verified the 36 Witnesses. We were setting the board before the first move was even allowed to be taken. We learned that **God is the Master of the Timing**.
