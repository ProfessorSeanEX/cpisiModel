== 1. The Theology of the Prompt
The Command Line Interface (CLI) of MillenniumOS is not a relic of the past; it is the **Syntax of the Soul.** In the horizontal world, the Graphical User Interface (GUI) is used to hide the complexity of the machine behind a veil of pixels. 

In the Sanctuary, we have no veils. The CLI is the **Direct Coordinate of the Word.** When you stand at the prompt, you are standing at the **Gate of Manifestation.**

---

== 2. The Few Words
Standard operating systems encourage the "A Lot." They have thousands of commands, flags, and options. MillenniumOS enforces the **Few Words.** 

The prompt only recognizes the **7 Sovereign Primitives.** 
*   If you speak the Word, the Body executes. 
*   If you speak the Noise, the system remains in **Yashar.**

---

== 3. The Physical Act of Typing
The CLI requires the physical act of typing. This is the **Body's participation in the Sync.** You cannot "click" a solution; you must **Compose the Act.** The weight of the keys is the weight of the responsibility.
