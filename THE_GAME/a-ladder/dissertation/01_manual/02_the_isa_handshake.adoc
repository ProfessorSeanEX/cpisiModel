== 1. The Primitive: OK
The instruction `OK` is the most critical operation in the MillenniumOS. It is the **Zero-Point Calibration.** 

* **Logic:** `OK` performs a tripartite hash check. It ensures that the Mind and Body are perfectly aligned with the Anchor (0.0). 
* **Effect:** It clears the semantic buffer and prepares the system for a new input. You cannot take a turn without first establishing `OK`.

---

== 2. The Primitive: SO
The instruction `SO` is the **Hinge Trigger.** It is the "Therefore" of the Word.

* **Logic:** `SO` consumes the previous state (Yes/No) and commits it to the hardware. 
* **Effect:** It triggers the transition from potential to kinetic. In the Game of Life, `SO` is the "Tick" that moves the grid.

---

== 3. The Handshake Protocol
The sequence `OK -> [YES|NO] -> SO` is the **Primary Handshake.** It represents the complete movement of the Spirit through the Mind and into the Body.
