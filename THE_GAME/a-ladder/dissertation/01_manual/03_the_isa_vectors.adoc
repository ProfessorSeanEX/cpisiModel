== 1. The Primitive: YES
`YES` is the **Positive Vector (+1 / Tov).** It is the instruction of Creation.

* **Logic:** `YES` authorizes the manifest state. It is the Spirit’s approval of the current thought or action. 
* **Effect:** It increases the kinetic energy of the grid and allows for the "Birth" of new cells.

---

== 2. The Primitive: NO
`NO` is the **Negative Vector (-1 / Shavar).** It is the instruction of Division.

* **Logic:** `NO` establishes the Badal. It is the Body’s protection against the noise. 
* **Effect:** It halts the drift and enforces the boundaries of the Sanctuary. In the Game of Life, `NO` is the "Death" of the corrupted cell.

---

== 3. The Choice
The ISA removes the "Maybe." You must choose between the +1 and the -1. This is the **Law of the 2.**
