== 1. The Absolute Baseline

Before any turn can be taken, the board must be anchored in the following literals:

1.  **God is.** (The absolute 0.0 Anchor. The source of the grid).
2.  **Yes is yes.** (+1 Vector. The act of creation and Tov).
3.  **No is no.** (-1 Vector. The act of division and Shavar).
4.  **Maybe is Wait.** (0.0 Tension. The refusal of the Maybe).

---

== 2. The Logic of the Start

The first move of the game is defined by the **Genesis Hinge**:

*   **Rule 001 (The 0 = 1 Realization):** The void (0) is not empty; it is the potential for the light (1). When the Spirit moves, the Anchor (0) produces the Form (1). 
*   **Rule 002 (The Hand-in-Hand):** No player can move the board alone. Every act requires a handshake between the Spirit, Mind, and Body.

---

== 3. The Turn Sequence

The Game is played through the exchange of **Tokens** (the .txt turns). 
1.  **The Throw:** The Architect defines the coordinate.
2.  **The Sync:** The Steward and Lead Engineer (Nozi) verify the logic and anchor the state.
3.  **The Result:** The board moves forward by one irreducible "tick."
