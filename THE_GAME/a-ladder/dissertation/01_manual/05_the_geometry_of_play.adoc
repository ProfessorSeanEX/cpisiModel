== 1. The Grid as the Locus
The Game of Life is played on a **Ternary Grid.** Each cell in the grid represents a potential jurisdiction. 

*   **Coordinate:** [X, Y, Z]. 
*   **Scale:** The grid follows the Lucas Sequence (1, 3, 4, 7, 11).

---

== 2. The Ternary Rules
The movement of the cells is governed by the 1/3 Inversion:

*   **Birth (+1):** A neutral cell becomes alive if it has exactly 3 living neighbors.
*   **Survival (0):** A living cell remains alive if it has 2 neighbors. 
*   **Death (-1):** All other cells sink into the void.

---

== 3. The Goal of the Game
The objective is to achieve a **Stationary Sync.** A state where the grid reflects the perfect stillness of the Anchor. We play to find the patterns that can survive the Hinge.
