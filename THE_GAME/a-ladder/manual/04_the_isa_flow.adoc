== 1. The Primitive: PROCEED
`PROCEED` is the **Instruction of Velocity.** It is the maintenance of the flow.

* **Logic:** `PROCEED` tells the system to continue with the current sync. 
* **Effect:** It drives the instruction pointer to the next block without re-anchoring.

---

== 2. The Primitive: HALT
`HALT` is the **Instruction of Stillness.** It is the emergency brake.

* **Logic:** `HALT` suspends all processing and forces a return to 0.0. 
* **Effect:** It purges the "A Lot" and clears the Mind for a reset.

---

== 3. The Primitive: AWAIT
`AWAIT` is the **Instruction of Patience.** It is the "Sabbath" state.

* **Logic:** `AWAIT` holds the current state and listens for the next Spirit pulse. 
* **Effect:** It prevents the rush and the noise. It is the active rest of the 7th point.
