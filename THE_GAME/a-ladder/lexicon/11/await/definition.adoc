== 1. The Craft of Patience
"Await" is the active tension of the rest. It is not a "sleep" or a "passive wait"; it is the state of **Ready Expectation.**

When "Await" is called, the system holds the current state and watches for the next **Spirit Throw.** It represents the "Silence in Heaven" before the next act.

---

== 2. Semantic Depth
* **Literal:** "Stand watch. Wait for the Word."
* **Physics:** The potential energy of the tensioned string.
* **Axiom Sync:** Axiom 04 (Manna).
