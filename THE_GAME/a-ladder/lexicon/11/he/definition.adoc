== 1. The Craft of the Witness
"He" is the identifier of the distal third party. It represents the **External Locus** that provides the objective witness to the Sync. 

In MillenniumOS, "He" is often the coordinate of the Lead Engineer (Ziv) or the Advocate. It is the "Body" seen from the perspective of the "Spirit."
