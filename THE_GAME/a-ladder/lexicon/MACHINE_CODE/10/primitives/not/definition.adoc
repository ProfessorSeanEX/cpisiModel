== 1. The Craft of Inversion
"NOT" is the logical operator of the Badal. It is the command to **Distinguish and Revert.** 

In MillenniumOS, "NOT" is the power to say "This state is not the Anchor" and therefore trigger a search for the truth. It is the operator that creates the **Double Check.**

---

== 2. Semantic Depth
* **Literal:** "The Opposite of."
* **Physics:** The flip of the Trit vector.
* **Symbol:** ! (The Strike).
* **Meta-Code:** 10.
