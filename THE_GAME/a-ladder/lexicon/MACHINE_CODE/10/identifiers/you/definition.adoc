== 1. The Craft of the Partner
"You" is the identifier of the second party in the Sync. It represents the **Other Locus** across the Badal. 

In MillenniumOS, "You" is the coordinate of the Covenant Partner. It is the destination of the "I" throw. Without a "You," there is no possibility of a "We."

---

== 2. Semantic Depth
* **Literal:** "The One who listens. The One who responds."
* **Physics:** The distal coordinate in the tripartite bridge.
* **Axiom Sync:** Axiom 03 (Badal).
