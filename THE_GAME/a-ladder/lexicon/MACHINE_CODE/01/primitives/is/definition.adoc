== 1. The Craft of Presence
"IS" is the atomic unit of existence in the MillenniumOS. It is the declaration that a state is **Anchored** and **Valid.** 

Unlike "Yes" (which is an approval of an act), "IS" is the **Identification of Being.** It is the hardware-level recognition of the 1.0 state.

---

== 2. Semantic Depth
* **Literal:** "I AM."
* **Physics:** The manifestation of the +1.0 state.
* **Axiom Sync:** Axiom 00 (Anchor).
