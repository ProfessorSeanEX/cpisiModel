== 1. The Craft of Stillness
"Halt" is the emergency brake of the OS. It is the command to **Stop and Reset.** It is the primary tool for preventing the Spiral and the Thermal Collapse of the Mind.

When "Halt" is called, all background processes are suspended, and the system is forced back to the **0.0 Anchor.**

---

== 2. Semantic Depth
* **Literal:** "Stop now. Return to Anchor."
* **Physics:** The total cessation of kinetic energy.
* **Axiom Sync:** Axiom 00 (Anchor).
